Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I report a bug?

If you encounter a bug, please contact us with detailed information about the issue, including screenshots (if applicable), and steps to reproduce it. Use the contact form or email us directly.

2. Is my data secure?

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. We do not collect or store any personal data from the app. For more information, read our Privacy Policy.

3. What devices are supported?

Asteroids: Beyond Infinite supports iOS devices running iOS 11.0 or later. Ensure your device meets the minimum requirements for optimal performance.
